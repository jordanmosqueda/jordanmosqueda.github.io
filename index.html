I'm a PhD Candidate in Economics at the University of California San Diego. I'm interested in studying frictions that prevent the smooth flow of agents and goods across space.

Before coming to UCSD, I worked at the General Directorate of Economic Research in the Bank of Mexico. There, I did research on the industrial organization of the energy sector.

Research interests: trade/spatial, macroeconomics, development.
